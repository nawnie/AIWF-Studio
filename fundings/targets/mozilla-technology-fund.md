## Application Draft

AIWF Studio is an open-source local AI workspace designed around user control. Instead of sending prompts, images, training data, and model operations to hosted services, AIWF Studio keeps workflows on the user's own machine. The architecture uses typed services, explicit model folders, optional engines, and supervised workers so users can understand what is running and where their data goes.

Mozilla funding would support privacy documentation, safer local model/download workflows, worker isolation, and security hardening for people who want capable local AI without surrendering their data to centralized platforms.
